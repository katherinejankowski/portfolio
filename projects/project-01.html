2.008: Yoyo Manufacturing

For the class 2.008, I worked on a team of 4 to design and manufacture 50 yoyos. After settling on a clock theme, we designed a yoyo that had two pressfits (one for the body and one for the minute/hour hand). The seconds hand was designed to be free to spin.
