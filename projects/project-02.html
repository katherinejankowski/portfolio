2.671: GC-MS Characterization of Resin Shelf Life

For the class 2.671, I chose to continue working on the research I did the previous summer. Over the summer, I helped develop a custom resin for DLP 3D printing parts with soluble supports. We noticed that the resin was degrading over time, and I wanted to quantify how long it would last (and potetnailly also determine a specific degradation mechanism). I chose to monitor the resin's levels of pyridine over time using GC-MS. My reasoning, data, and findings are presented in the paper and poster below.
